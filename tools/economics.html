Read this before you trust the number. The two dials that move everything are mean views/account (a content-quality / hit-rate function — where proven-formula content earns its keep) and blended RPM (a stacking function, not a platform function). Churn is modeled as an uptime drag (effective accounts = accounts × (1 − churn/2)); months 1–2 are a warm-up ramp, not steady state. The honest spread on a real fleet is roughly ±10× until you run the two-week probe and replace assumptions with measured hit-rate and survivability. Numbers are a lens, not a promise.
